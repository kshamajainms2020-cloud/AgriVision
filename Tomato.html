Diseased Tomato Leaf

1. Early Blight

  • Cause: Fungus Alternaria solani
  • Infection: Dark concentric rings on lower leaves
  • Leaf Effect: Yellowing, drying, and falling off

2. Late Blight

  • Cause: Fungus Phytophthora infestans
  • Infection: Water-soaked lesions on leaves & stems
  • Leaf Effect: Rapid wilting & rotting

3. Leaf Curl Virus

  • Cause: Whitefly transmitted virus
  • Infection: Virus spreads via insect vectors
  • Leaf Effect: Curling, thickening, stunted growth

4. Bacterial Spot

  • Cause: Xanthomonas campestris
  • Infection: Small water-soaked spots
  • Leaf Effect: Necrotic lesions, premature drop
